SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY

Last updated: 10 July 2026

FORGE sells 100% digital products. There is no physical shipping involved — everything is delivered electronically to the email address you provide at checkout.

1. How Delivery Works

Once your payment is successfully completed via Razorpay, your order is delivered as follows:

ProductDelivery MethodDelivery Time
E-BooksPDF sent to your emailInstant (within minutes)
Workout PlansPDF + tracker sent to your emailWithin 24 hours
Online CoachingOnboarding email + intake formWithin 24–48 hours

2. Delivery Area

Since all products are digital, they are available for purchase and delivery anywhere in India, and internationally, as long as you have a valid email address.

3. Didn't Receive Your Order?

If you haven't received your product within the timeframes above:

  • Check your Spam, Junk, or Promotions folder
  • Confirm the email address entered at checkout was correct
  • Email us at forgebody@gmail.com with your name and payment reference — we'll resend it right away

4. Re-Download Access

All e-books and workout plans come with lifetime re-download access. If you lose your files, contact us and we'll re-send your download link at no extra cost.

5. Contact Us

For any delivery-related issues, reach out at forgebody@gmail.com.
